Tribute to A Woman!

A beautiful Woman

A storm withheld, a sun which never sets, The love of nature and kindness at its best, The world knows how you quest, Oh Lord! you made a woman so perfect! She plays so many roles, A daughter, a mother, a wife and best friend, Wanting always the peoples worry around her to end, Standing up for her rights and never gets bend, Oh Lord! you made a woman as a circle with no mend! The highs and lows of her life, the criticism for having her own choice, what not she faced being a woman, Harassment, forced-marriages and domestic violence, Yet she smiles with all her broken pieces together, Oh Lord! You made a woman with no fear She is out there to rule the world and make a mark, The history has already been embarked, The light she holds that forever sparks, She unwinds the passion to carve, Oh Lord! How you made a woman with so much love!

Valentine

Do we need a Valentines Day?

Love knows no boundaries, love is limitless, love is self-less above all Love is unconditional! Now the question on the table is 'do we require a special day to celebtate love and define as valentines day' may be yes or may be no. People who are in love can add a little spark to their routine life whereas people who are still waiting for there soulmate can continue figuring out what to do on this day.

Pros of Valentines day : 1. An excitement fulfilled day for all the lovers around the world. 2. Gifts!Gifts!Gifts! who would not want a special gift from their loved ones. 3. Romance and love will be in the air. 4. It is so much better to spread love than hatredness. 5. Theme parties and events which will definately add spice to the regular life.
